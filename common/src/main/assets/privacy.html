Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the security of your personal information is extremely important to us. Your information will not be given to anyone, period. Sensitive information like credit card details are NOT stored on our servers. They will be processed only through our trusted secure payment partner PAYU, Paytm, BHIM etc.

We collect some information like these:

Email Id

We hate spam as much as you do. We will email about your order, when you place an order with us. We do not sell your email address to other parties. We will also send you newsletters and you can opt out anytime.

Personal Information

When you place your order we collect some information like your name, shipping and billing address, phone number and email address. This information is used to provide with support for processing your order only. We will never sell your personal information to other parties.

Anonymous Information

Some non-personal information is also collected by some of tools we use like browser type, ISP etc. It is mainly for non-personal analytics about the shopping behaviour - it is not used for targeting any specific user but rather all our shoppers as a whole - to know trends / overall likes / shopping pain points etc. The information is completely anonymous and is collected by pretty much every site that you visit.

Cookies

We use cookies - again like all e-commerce sites. They do not store any personal information, but they help in other technical stuff like keeping items in your cart etc.

Security

Your security is extremely important to us and we do everything to ensure that your information is as safe as possible. No outsider can view your information.

Anti-Counterfeiting Policy

Products offered for sale on Retailersbook must be authentic. The sale of counterfeit products is strictly prohibited. Failure to abide by this policy may result in loss of selling privileges, funds being withheld, and destruction of inventory in our possession.

It is each seller’s and supplier’s responsibility to source, sell, and fulfill only authentic products. Prohibited products include bootlegs, fakes, or pirated copies of products or content; products that have been illegally replicated, reproduced, or manufactured; and products that infringe another party’s intellectual property rights. If you sell or supply inauthentic products, we may immediately suspend or terminate your Retailersbook selling account (and any related accounts) and destroy any inauthentic products in our fulfillment centers at your expense. In addition, we do not pay sellers until we are confident our customers have received the authentic products they ordered. We may withhold payments if we determine that a Retailersbook account has been used to sell inauthentic goods, commit fraud, or engage in other illegal activity.

We work with manufacturers, rights holders, content owners, vendors, and sellers to improve the ways we detect and prevent inauthentic products from reaching our customers. As a result, we remove suspect listings based on our own review of products. We also work with rights holders and law enforcement worldwide to take and support legal action against sellers and suppliers that knowingly violate this policy and harm our customers. In addition to criminal fines and imprisonment, sellers and suppliers of inauthentic products may face civil penalties including the loss of any amounts received from the sale of inauthentic products, the damage or harm sustained by the rights holders, statutory and other damages, and attorney’s fees.
